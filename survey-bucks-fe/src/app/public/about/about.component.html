About Our Platform

We're building South Africa's most honest and rewarding survey platform, connecting local voices with businesses that need authentic insights.

Our Journey

We're a new South African startup launching in 2024 with a simple but important mission: create a survey platform that's genuinely fair, transparent, and designed for the South African market.

We noticed that most survey platforms either don't serve South Africans well or aren't transparent about earnings and opportunities. We're building something different - a platform that grows with our community.

While we're just getting started, we're committed to honest communication about our progress, realistic earning expectations, and building genuine value for both participants and businesses.
